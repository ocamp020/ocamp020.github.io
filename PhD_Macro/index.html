EC9604 Advanced Macroeconomics

A searchable web edition of the lecture notes, organized around recursive methods, heterogeneity, firm dynamics, and input-output networks. The PDFs remain available, but the notes now live in a section-by-section course site that is much easier to browse.

Browse the Notes Open the Notes PDF

What the website improves

Students can move through the course by topic rather than scrolling through a long PDF. Theorems, proofs, examples, and input-output diagrams each have their own page anchors and are easier to revisit during problem-set work.

How to use it

Start with the notes browser, navigate by part and section, and use the PDFs when a printable version is more convenient. The web notes preserve math and turn the input-output figures into browser-rendered diagrams.
